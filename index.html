Welcome To Our Heart

As an attractive business and science location with a high quality of life, Wildau is above all a place of departure from the European center to the European top. Wildau is traditionally a place of work and intelligence. "Personalities, not principles, move the times." Oscar Wilde

Here you can work successfully and live relatively well. Education is a focus here. You can learn and develop well throughout your life here.

Wildau is a place with nature, modern leisure opportunities and excellent shopping opportunities. One of the largest and most modern shopping centers in Europe is located in Wildau.

But Wildau is also a special place for young people. There are more than 4,000 students. The daycare centers and schools here also have a special quality.

Come and see for yourself! Get moving and move Wildau forward with us!
